Memory Game Instructions

You will be shown a board of cards, each with a symbol on their back.

On each turn, you will click on two cards to flip them over and reveal their symbol.

If the symbols match, the cards will stay flipped, and you will continue your turn.

If the symbols do not match, the cards will turn back over, and you will be given a new turn.

The game is over when you match all of the cards.

Try to match them in the fewest turns possible!
